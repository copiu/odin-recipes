Chantal's New York Style Cheesecake

Description

There's no doubt cheesecake is a classic, and there's nothing any more classic than the New York style cheesecake! Easy to make and delicious, There's enough to share with everyone, or enough only to yourself (we don't blame you). This New York style cheesecake by Chantal is a delectable choice of dessert to end the day!

Ingredients

  • 15 graham crackers, crushed
  • 2 tablespoons butter, melted
  • 4 (8 ounce) packages cream cheese
  • 1 1/2 cups white sugar
  • 3/4 cup milk
  • 4 eggs
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease a 9-inch spring form pan.
  2. Mix graham cracker crumbs and melted butter together in a medium bowl until well combined; press onto the bottom of the prepared pan to make a packed crust.
  3. Mix cream cheese and sugar together in a large bowl until smooth. Blend in milk, then mix in eggs, one at a time, until just combined. Add sour cream, flour, and vanilla; mix until smooth. Pour filling onto prepared crust.
  4. Bake in preheated oven for 1 hour. Turn the oven off; let cheesecake cool in the oven with the door closed for 5 to 6 hours to prevent cracking.
  5. Chill in the refrigerator until serving.
